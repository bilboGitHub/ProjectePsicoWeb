Production Lead

Location Annapolis

Summary

The Production Lead position is multi-tasking warehouse and roasting floor linchpin of our operation, and it requires hard work and dedication to fulfill our deadlines and commitment to customers.

Job Description

compensation: $29 – 34K
employment type: full-time

Ceremony Coffee Roasters is a quality-driven roaster/retailer pursuing all things good in coffee. In our hard work together with partners and customers, we are about authentic relationships, shared knowledge, and beautiful coffee experiences.The production lead job entails a lot of responsibility and requires hard, team-focused work and a commitment to our customers to represent our coffee in the best possible way. Your job’s primary role is keeping the production area running efficiently, with an extremely high level of attention to detail. You will work directly with the production, roasting, and admin teams toward this end.

Core Responsibilities
Maintain a high standard of work and cooperation in all production and warehouse needs to ensure a positive experience for yourself, our customers, and co-workers.

Essential Functions
Include bagging coffee, labeling bags, loading coffee bins, blending coffee, weighing green coffee, stocking shelves, loading the van, weekly inventory, cleaning, light machinery maintenance, keeping labels consistent and accurate, overseeing order fulfillment, and assisting in other areas when necessary.

Benefits
Include a health/dental/vision insurance contribution, earned paid vacation, holidays and sick leave, 401K w/ company match, wellness benefit, potential for profit-sharing annually, discounted Ceremony products, and opportunities for career advancement.

Qualifications and Requirements
The right candidate will be able to work full-time, Monday-Friday business hours, be prompt and reliable, desire to go the extra mile within a small team, be comfortable on their feet, and able to lift over 60 lbs. You should also have a solid employment history and references. Additionally, the most desirable employee will be a good communicator, open to offering and accepting constructive feedback, capable of maintaining calm while working quickly under pressure, interested in leaning new systems/methods, have good organizational and time-management skills, show good collaboration skills, and demonstrate solid core values (honesty, integrity, respect). A love of coffee and a desire to learn more are pluses!
